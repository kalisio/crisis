# Go further

This section details some of the most advanced features available in Akt'n'Map:

  • the workflows to make your events interactive with fieldworkers,
  • the logbook (i.e. archive or history) to ensure a complete traceability of your events with relevant statistics,
  • the catalog to integrate your own cartographic data in the maps,
  • alerts to be automatically notified when environmental hazards are detected,
  • plans to help you manage your risky situations.
